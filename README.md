You start to become forgetful, unsure whether you turned off the lights or locked the door before leaving, and you end up repeatedly checking, wasting a lot of time. You can't help but feel anxious about everything. You will need things to help you escape reality because you can't avoid the thoughts in your own mind. You don't know where they come from or when they will leave. However, anxiety stems from fear; if you can identify this fear, you will feel better. Don't try to control what you can't control. Don't worry about what will happen next. The world is ever-changing and unpredictable, which only increases your anxiety. If something does happen, believe that you can handle it, focus on the present reality, and be the best version of yourself.</p>

            <div class="video-caption">视频6：精神障碍总览 (Overview of Mental Disorders)</div>
            <div class="video-description">
                <p>1.创伤后应激障碍，你在一场车祸中醒来，你失去了你的至亲，本来并没有什么，但在一周后的康复后，你看到车就会不自觉想到那就事，听到鸣笛声，碰撞声，你无法终止它们，同时伴随呼吸困难，陷入恐慌，流汗，紧张，甚至晕厥过去。这是大脑的防御机制，让你永远无法忘记那件事。<br>
                2.躁郁症，你可能昨天还站在世界顶峰，你可以做到任何事，但瞬间，你会失去所有力气，失去干劲，身体像灌了铅，这种剧烈的情绪波动不想普通的波动，他会让你接近崩溃，甚至自杀。<br>
                3.焦虑症，它会让人持续陷入恐慌，明明身边没有危险，却会立马进入逃跑或战斗的状态，无时无刻不担心未来的事情。<br>
                4.双向情感障碍，这是一个你无法用意志力以及无法选择的病症，必须接受专业治疗。<br>
                5.抑郁症，它起始于持续性的深度的心情低落，对抗深度抑郁的感觉，就像摸黑在风暴里开船，你整个人会十分暴躁，失去希望，让你精疲力尽，迎来体重的剧烈波动，甚至突然的自杀想法。<br>
                6.注意力缺陷多动症，就像大脑在疯狂工作不停下，你的大脑会让你无法集中注意力，更加冲动。<br>
                7.饮食失调，这是因为你对自身身材的认知出现偏差，或进入节食的重度循环或者暴食的重复循环，在暴饮暴食后选择催吐，滥用泻药等，这个病症也会让你患上抑郁症和焦虑症的概率大幅上升。<br>
                8.强迫症，它会让你产生挥之不去的念头，重复的洗手，过度清洁，重复检查物品，试图通过这些方式缓解自己的焦虑。<br>
                9.自闭症谱系障碍，它会影响患者的沟通方式，社交互动，甚至感知世界的方式。同时，每个患者都有不同的症状，这让其更加捉摸不透。他们最特别的点在于不知道如何处理事情，不习惯眼神交流，容易误解社交信号。<br>
                10.睡眠障碍，这是个很严重的问题，它不仅仅是单纯的睡不好，它剥夺了你的睡眠，让人陷入崩溃，它可能会找上任何人。<br>
                11.偏执型人格障碍，这是一种精神疾病，最典型的表现就是过度疑神疑鬼，总觉得有人要害自己，这种猜疑会被拉满到极致，它会让你觉得更加容易被冒犯，甚至分不清现实和幻想，甚至发展成为精神分裂。<br>
                12.精神分裂症，很多人小时候都有自己的一个幻想伙伴，你或许知道他是假的，但是他一直陪着你甚至长大。你的大脑会创造出幻想，虚假却又现实无比。你的大脑处理时间的功能被扭曲，分不清过去现在未来。<br>
                13.阅读障碍，一个让人摸不清头脑的病状，阅读障碍患者甚至认不清字母，这个病症直接影响到了大脑负责处理文字的区域，这会让你看到的文字变得乱七八糟，特别难懂。但是阅读障碍和智力的高低并没有直接影响。</p>
                <p class="en">1. Post-traumatic stress disorder: You wake up in a car accident, losing your loved one. Normally, nothing happens, but after a week of recovery, you can't help but think about the car when you see it. You hear horns and crashes, you can't stop them, and experience difficulty breathing, panic, sweating, nervousness, and even fainting. This is the brain's defense mechanism, making it impossible to forget that event.<br>
                2. Bipolar disorder: You might have been at the top of the world just yesterday, able to do anything, but in an instant, you lose all your strength and drive, your body feels like it's filled with lead. These intense emotional fluctuations are not ordinary, and they can bring you close to collapse, even suicide.<br>
                3. Anxiety disorder: It causes people to continuously panic; even when there is no danger nearby, they immediately enter a state of running away or fighting, constantly worrying about the future.<br>
                4. Bisopathic Disorder, a condition you cannot rely on willpower or choice, and requires professional treatment.<br>
                5. Depression, which begins with persistent, deep depression. Fighting deep depression is like sailing in the dark; you become irritable, lose hope, exhausted, experience drastic weight fluctuations, and even sudden suicidal thoughts.<br>
                6. Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is like your brain working nonstop, making it hard for you to concentrate and become more impulsive.<br>
                7. Eating disorders: This is due to a misunderstanding of your own body shape, or you may enter a heavy cycle of dieting or repeated binge eating. After binge eating, you choose to induce vomiting or abuse laxatives. This condition also greatly increases your risk of depression and anxiety.<br>
                8. Obsessive-compulsive disorder: it causes persistent thoughts, repeated handwashing, excessive cleaning, and repeatedly checking items, all trying to ease your anxiety.<br>
                9. Autism spectrum disorder, which affects the way patients communicate, interact socially, and even perceive the world. At the same time, each patient has different symptoms, making them even more elusive. Their most unique trait is not knowing how to handle things, not accustomed to eye contact, and easily misinterpreting social signals.<br>
                10. Sleep disorders are a serious problem. It's not just about poor sleep—they rob you of your sleep, drive you to a breakdown, and can affect anyone.<br>
                11. Paranoid personality disorder, a mental illness characterized by excessive paranoia, always feeling someone is trying to harm you. This suspicion can be pushed to the extreme, making you feel more easily offended, unable to distinguish reality from fantasy, and may even develop into schizophrenia.<br>
                12. Schizophrenia: Many people have a fantasy companion as a child. You might know it's fake, but they have always accompanied you and even grown up. Your brain creates fantasies—false yet incredibly real. Your brain's ability to process time is distorted, making it hard to distinguish past, present, and future.<br>
                13. Dyslexia, a condition that leaves people puzzled, can make it so that patients cannot even recognize letters. This condition directly affects the areas of the brain responsible for processing text, which makes the words you see appear messy and particularly difficult to understand. However, dyslexia does not have a direct impact on intelligence.</p>
